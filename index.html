      How to Play GoldRushClick

      Welcome to GoldRushClick! Here’s how to get started:

      • Click for Gold: Click the "⛏️ Click for Gold!" text to earn coins.
      • Buy Upgrades: Spend coins in the Shop to increase your Digs per Second (DPS). Start with a Basic Pickaxe (10 coins) and work up to a Gold Vault (2000 coins)!
      • Premium Rush: Pay $1 to boost your DPS by 2—perfect for a quick jump.
      • Prestige: Once you hit 1000 coins, reset your progress for a 1.5x multiplier—stack it up for huge gains!
      • Refer Friends: Share your referral code (in Stats). When they use it, you both get 50 coins.
      • Events: Watch for "Gold Rush" (2x coins, 30s) or "Coin Shower" (10x coins, 10s) events—stack them for crazy rewards!
      • Achievements: Earn badges like "100 Coins" or "Click Addict" as you play.
      • Leaderboard: Climb the ranks—top 5 players show off their coins and multipliers.
